# Shipping Policy

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## Shipping Information

We are committed to delivering your orders quickly and safely. This Shipping Policy outlines our shipping methods, delivery times, and related information.

## Shipping Methods

### Standard Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Cost**: Calculated at checkout based on weight and destination
- **Tracking**: Included with all standard shipments

### Express Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: 2-3 business days
- **Cost**: Additional fee applies
- **Tracking**: Real-time tracking available

### Overnight Shipping
- **Delivery Time**: Next business day (order by 2 PM EST)
- **Cost**: Premium shipping fee
- **Tracking**: Priority tracking included

## Shipping Destinations

We currently ship to:
- All 50 United States
- International shipping available to select countries
- Shipping restrictions may apply to certain products

## Processing Time

- **Standard Orders**: Processed within 1-2 business days
- **Custom Orders**: May require 3-5 business days for processing
- **Holiday Periods**: Processing may take longer during peak seasons

## Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

- Package weight and dimensions
- Shipping destination
- Selected shipping method
- Product type and special handling requirements

Free shipping is available for orders over $50 (standard shipping only).

## Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive:

- Shipping confirmation email with tracking number
- Real-time tracking updates
- Estimated delivery date
- Delivery notifications

## Delivery

### Delivery Address
- Ensure your delivery address is correct at checkout
- We are not responsible for orders delivered to incorrect addresses provided by customers
- Signature may be required for high-value items

### Delivery Issues
If you experience delivery problems:

- Contact us within 7 days of expected delivery date
- Provide your order number and tracking information
- We will investigate and resolve the issue promptly

## International Shipping

### Customs and Duties
- International customers are responsible for customs fees and import duties
- These charges are not included in the order total
- Delivery may be delayed due to customs processing

### International Delivery Times
- Varies by country: 7-21 business days
- Subject to customs clearance
- Tracking available for most destinations

## Undeliverable Packages

Packages may be returned to us if:

- Address is incorrect or incomplete
- Package is unclaimed after delivery attempts
- Recipient refuses delivery

In such cases, we will contact you to arrange reshipment (additional fees may apply).

## Shipping Restrictions

Some items cannot be shipped to certain locations due to:

- Legal restrictions
- Product type (hazardous materials, perishables)
- Destination country regulations

We will notify you if your order cannot be shipped to your location.

## Lost or Damaged Packages

### Lost Packages
If your package is lost in transit:

- Contact us immediately
- We will file a claim with the shipping carrier
- Replacement or refund will be provided once confirmed lost

### Damaged Packages
If your package arrives damaged:

- Take photos of the damaged package and contents
- Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
- We will arrange replacement or refund

## Shipping Holidays

We do not ship on:

- New Year's Day
- Independence Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Christmas Day

Orders placed on holidays will be processed the next business day.
