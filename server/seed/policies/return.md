# Return & Refund Policy

**Last Updated: January 2024**

## Return Policy Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This Return & Refund Policy explains our procedures for returns, exchanges, and refunds.

## Return Eligibility

### Eligible Items
- Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
- Products must be unused, unopened, and in original packaging
- Items must be in the same condition as received
- Original receipt or order confirmation required

### Non-Returnable Items
- Personalized or custom-made products
- Perishable goods
- Intimate or sanitary goods
- Digital products (software, e-books, downloadable content)
- Gift cards
- Items damaged by customer misuse
- Products without original packaging

## Return Process

### Step 1: Initiate Return
- Log into your account or contact customer service
- Provide order number and reason for return
- Receive return authorization and instructions

### Step 2: Package Items
- Use original packaging when possible
- Include all original accessories, manuals, and tags
- Securely package items to prevent damage during transit

### Step 3: Ship Return
- Use provided return shipping label (if applicable)
- Ship to address provided in return instructions
- Retain shipping receipt for tracking

## Return Shipping

### Free Returns
- Free returns available for defective or incorrect items
- Prepaid return labels provided for eligible returns

### Customer-Paid Returns
- Standard return shipping costs apply for change of mind returns
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless item was defective or incorrect

## Refund Processing

### Refund Methods
- Refunds issued to original payment method
- Processing time: 5-10 business days after we receive returned item
- Bank processing may take additional 3-5 business days

### Refund Amount
- Full refund for eligible returns
- Original shipping costs refunded for defective/incorrect items
- Original shipping costs not refunded for change of mind returns

## Exchange Policy

### Exchanges Available
- Size exchanges (subject to availability)
- Color exchanges (subject to availability)
- Product exchanges for items of equal or greater value

### Exchange Process
- Contact customer service to initiate exchange
- Return original item following standard return process
- New item shipped once return is received and processed

## Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item:

1. **Contact Us Immediately**: Within 48 hours of delivery
2. **Provide Evidence**: Photos of damage or defect
3. **Quick Resolution**: We will expedite replacement or refund
4. **No Return Required**: For certain items, replacement sent without return

## Wrong Item Received

If you receive the wrong item:

- Contact us immediately
- We will arrange correct item shipment
- Return shipping for wrong item is free
- Full refund available if correct item is out of stock

## Late Returns

Returns received after 30 days:

- May be accepted at our discretion
- Store credit may be issued instead of refund
- Subject to restocking fee (up to 20%)

## Restocking Fees

Restocking fees may apply to:

- Electronics (10% of item value)
- Large items requiring special handling
- Items returned without original packaging
- Custom or personalized items (if returnable)

## Refund Timeline

- **Return Received**: 1-2 business days to process
- **Inspection**: 2-3 business days
- **Refund Issued**: 1-2 business days
- **Bank Processing**: 3-5 business days

**Total Time**: Approximately 7-12 business days from return receipt to refund credit

## Store Credit

Instead of refund, you may choose:

- Store credit (issued immediately)
- Exchange for different product
- Credit valid for 12 months from issue date

## Holiday Returns

Extended return period during holiday season:

- Items purchased November 1 - December 24
- Return deadline: January 31 of following year
- Standard return conditions apply

## International Returns

International customers:

- Responsible for return shipping costs
- May be subject to customs fees
- Refund issued in original currency
- Processing may take additional time
