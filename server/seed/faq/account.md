# Account & Profile FAQ

## Account Management

### How do I create an account?
Creating an account is easy! Click on "Sign Up" or "Register" on our homepage. You can sign up using:
- Email address and password
- Phone number and OTP
- Google account
- Facebook account

### Is creating an account free?
Yes, creating an account is completely free. There are no charges or subscription fees for having an account.

### Can I have multiple accounts?
We recommend having one account per person. Multiple accounts may be flagged for security reasons. If you need multiple accounts for business purposes, contact our support team.

### How do I delete my account?
To delete your account, go to "Account Settings" > "Privacy" > "Delete Account". Note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure to cancel any pending orders first.

## Login & Security

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email or phone number, and we'll send you a reset link or OTP. Follow the instructions to create a new password.

### How do I change my password?
Log in to your account, go to "Account Settings" > "Security" > "Change Password". Enter your current password and set a new one.

### What makes a strong password?
A strong password should:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include numbers and special characters
- Not be easily guessable (avoid personal information)

### Can I use the same password for multiple accounts?
For security reasons, we recommend using a unique password for your account. This protects you if one of your accounts is compromised.

### How do I enable two-factor authentication?
Go to "Account Settings" > "Security" > "Two-Factor Authentication" and follow the setup instructions. This adds an extra layer of security to your account.

## Profile Information

### How do I update my personal information?
Log in to your account, go to "My Account" > "Profile Settings", and update your name, email, phone number, or date of birth.

### Can I change my registered email address?
Yes, go to "Account Settings" > "Profile" > "Change Email". You'll need to verify the new email address through a confirmation link.

### How do I update my phone number?
Go to "Account Settings" > "Profile" > "Change Phone Number". Enter your new number and verify it with an OTP.

### What information is required for my profile?
Basic information includes your name, email, and phone number. Additional details like date of birth and gender are optional but help us personalize your experience.

## Address Management

### How do I add a new delivery address?
Go to "My Account" > "Addresses" > "Add New Address". Fill in your complete address details, including pin code, and save it.

### Can I have multiple saved addresses?
Yes, you can save multiple addresses for home, office, or other locations. Select the appropriate address during checkout.

### How do I edit or delete a saved address?
Go to "My Account" > "Addresses", click on the address you want to edit or delete, and make your changes.

### What if my address is not in your delivery list?
If your pin code is not serviceable, contact our customer support. We're constantly expanding our delivery network and may be able to arrange delivery.

## Account Preferences

### How do I manage my notification preferences?
Go to "Account Settings" > "Notifications" to choose which notifications you want to receive via email, SMS, or push notifications.

### Can I unsubscribe from promotional emails?
Yes, you can unsubscribe from promotional emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any promotional email, or by managing your preferences in "Account Settings" > "Notifications".

### How do I change my language preference?
Go to "Account Settings" > "Preferences" > "Language" and select your preferred language. The website will display in your chosen language.

## Order History & Tracking

### How do I view my order history?
Log in to your account and go to "My Orders". You'll see all your past and current orders with details like order date, status, and tracking information.

### Can I reorder a previous purchase?
Yes, go to "My Orders", find the order you want to reorder, and click "Buy Again". The items will be added to your cart.

### How do I track my current orders?
Go to "My Orders" and click on any active order to see detailed tracking information, including current status and estimated delivery date.

### What order statuses can I see?
Order statuses include:
- Order Placed
- Confirmed
- Processing
- Shipped
- Out for Delivery
- Delivered
- Cancelled
- Returned

## Wishlist & Saved Items

### How do I add items to my wishlist?
Click the heart icon on any product page to add it to your wishlist. You can also add items from search results or category pages.

### Where can I see my wishlist?
Go to "My Account" > "Wishlist" to see all your saved items. You can move items to cart or remove them from here.

### Can I share my wishlist with others?
Yes, you can share your wishlist by generating a shareable link from the wishlist page. This is great for sharing gift ideas!

### How many items can I save in my wishlist?
There's no limit to the number of items you can save in your wishlist. Save as many as you like!

## Account Issues

### I'm unable to log in. What should I do?
Try these steps:
1. Check if Caps Lock is on
2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
3. Try resetting your password
4. Use the "Login with OTP" option
5. Contact customer support if issues persist

### My account is locked. How do I unlock it?
If your account is locked due to multiple failed login attempts, wait 30 minutes and try again. For security-related locks, contact customer support.

### I'm not receiving verification emails. What should I do?
Check your spam/junk folder. If you still don't receive emails, verify your email address is correct, or contact customer support to resend verification emails.

### Can I merge two accounts?
If you have accidentally created multiple accounts, contact customer support with details of both accounts. We'll help you merge them if possible.
