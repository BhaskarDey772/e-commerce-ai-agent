# General FAQ

## Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" button on our website. You'll need to provide your email address, phone number, and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration.

### Can I use my account on multiple devices?
Yes, you can access your account from any device - mobile, tablet, or computer. Simply log in with your registered email and password.

### How do I reset my password?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.

### How do I update my profile information?
Log in to your account, go to "My Account" > "Profile Settings", and update your personal information, address, or contact details.

## Orders & Payments

### How do I place an order?
Browse our products, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Enter your shipping address, select a payment method, and confirm your order. You'll receive an order confirmation email with all details.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, net banking, and cash on delivery (COD) for eligible orders.

### Is it safe to pay online?
Yes, we use secure payment gateways with SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We never store your complete card details on our servers.

### Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel your order before it's shipped. Go to "My Orders" in your account, select the order, and click "Cancel Order". Once shipped, you can return the product as per our return policy.

### How do I track my order?
You can track your order by logging into your account and going to "My Orders". You'll also receive SMS and email updates with tracking information and delivery status.

### What is the estimated delivery time?
Delivery time varies by location and product availability. Generally, orders are delivered within 3-7 business days. You'll see the estimated delivery date at checkout and in your order confirmation.

## Shipping & Delivery

### Do you ship to all locations?
We ship to most locations across India. Enter your pin code during checkout to check if we deliver to your area.

### What are the shipping charges?
Shipping charges vary based on your location and order value. Free shipping is available on orders above a certain amount. Check the shipping charges at checkout before placing your order.

### Can I change my delivery address after placing an order?
You can change the delivery address if your order hasn't been shipped yet. Contact our customer support team or update it from "My Orders" section.

### What if I'm not available to receive the delivery?
If you're not available, our delivery partner will attempt delivery again. You can also reschedule the delivery or pick it up from the nearest delivery center. Contact customer support for assistance.

### Do you offer express delivery?
Yes, we offer express delivery for select products and locations. You'll see the express delivery option at checkout if available for your order.

## Returns & Refunds

### What is your return policy?
You can return most products within 7-10 days of delivery, depending on the product category. Products must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags and accessories intact.

### How do I return a product?
Log into your account, go to "My Orders", select the order, and click "Return". Fill in the return reason and submit. We'll arrange a pickup or provide a return shipping label.

### How long does it take to process a refund?
Once we receive and verify the returned product, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

### Can I exchange a product?
Yes, you can exchange products for a different size, color, or variant. Select "Exchange" instead of "Return" in your order details. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

### What if I receive a damaged or wrong product?
If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact our customer support immediately. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost.

## Products & Shopping

### How do I search for products?
Use the search bar at the top of the page to search by product name, brand, or category. You can also browse by categories and use filters to narrow down your search.

### Are the product images accurate?
We strive to provide accurate product images, but colors may vary slightly due to screen settings. Please refer to the product description for detailed specifications.

### Do you offer product warranties?
Warranty information is provided for each product on its product page. Most electronics come with manufacturer warranties. Check the product details for warranty terms.

### Can I see product reviews before buying?
Yes, you can read customer reviews and ratings on each product page. Reviews help you make informed purchasing decisions.

### What if a product is out of stock?
If a product is out of stock, you can click "Notify Me" to receive an email when it's back in stock. You can also check similar products or browse alternatives.

## Discounts & Offers

### How do I apply a coupon code?
Enter your coupon code in the "Apply Coupon" field during checkout. Valid codes will be applied automatically, and you'll see the discount reflected in your order total.

### Where can I find active offers and discounts?
Check our homepage, special offers page, or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on the latest deals, sales, and promotional offers.

### Can I combine multiple coupon codes?
Generally, only one coupon code can be applied per order. However, some special promotions may allow combining offers. Check the terms and conditions of each coupon.

## Security & Privacy

### How do you protect my personal information?
We use industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, to protect your personal and payment information. We never share your data with third parties without your consent.

### Can I shop without creating an account?
Yes, you can browse and add items to your cart without an account. However, you'll need to create an account to complete the purchase and track your orders.

### How do you use my data?
We use your data to process orders, improve our services, and send you relevant offers (with your consent). You can manage your privacy preferences in your account settings.

## Technical Issues

### The website is not loading properly. What should I do?
Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or use a different browser. If the issue persists, contact our technical support team.

### I'm having trouble with the mobile app. What can I do?
Update the app to the latest version, restart your device, or reinstall the app. If problems continue, contact our support team with details about the issue.

### How do I enable notifications?
Go to your account settings and enable push notifications. You can also manage notification preferences for order updates, offers, and promotions.
