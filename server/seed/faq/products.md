# Product Related FAQ

## Product Information

### How accurate are product descriptions?
We strive to provide accurate and detailed product descriptions. All information is verified from manufacturers and sellers. If you notice any discrepancies, please contact our customer support.

### Can I see product specifications?
Yes, detailed product specifications are available on each product page. Click on the "Specifications" tab to view technical details, dimensions, features, and other relevant information.

### Are product ratings and reviews genuine?
Yes, all reviews are from verified purchasers. We have strict policies against fake reviews and regularly monitor and remove any suspicious content.

### How do I compare products?
You can compare products by adding them to your wishlist or using the "Compare" feature on product pages. This helps you see side-by-side differences in features, prices, and specifications.

### What if a product doesn't match its description?
If a product doesn't match its description, you can return it within the return period for a full refund. Contact customer support for assistance with the return process.

## Product Availability

### How do I know if a product is in stock?
Product availability is shown on each product page. "In Stock" means the product is available for immediate shipping. "Out of Stock" items can be added to your wishlist for notifications.

### Can I pre-order products?
Yes, select products may be available for pre-order. You'll see a "Pre-order" option with the expected availability date. Payment is processed when the product is ready to ship.

### What does "Limited Stock" mean?
"Limited Stock" indicates that only a few units are available. We recommend ordering quickly to avoid missing out.

### How often is inventory updated?
Inventory is updated in real-time. However, during high-traffic periods like sales, there may be slight delays in stock updates.

## Product Quality & Authenticity

### Are products genuine and authentic?
Yes, we only sell genuine products from authorized sellers and manufacturers. We have strict quality control measures and partnerships with verified suppliers.

### What if I receive a counterfeit product?
We guarantee 100% genuine products. If you suspect a counterfeit, contact us immediately with photos and details. We'll investigate and provide a full refund plus compensation.

### Do products come with original packaging?
Yes, all products are delivered in their original manufacturer packaging, unless otherwise specified (e.g., open-box items).

### Are refurbished products available?
Some categories may have refurbished or open-box products. These are clearly marked with their condition and come with appropriate warranties.

## Product Warranty & Service

### What warranty do products come with?
Warranty information is displayed on each product page. Most electronics come with manufacturer warranties ranging from 6 months to 2 years, depending on the product.

### How do I claim warranty?
Contact the manufacturer's service center directly using the warranty card provided, or reach out to our customer support for assistance with warranty claims.

### Do you provide installation services?
Installation services are available for select products like air conditioners, TVs, and home appliances. You can add installation service during checkout for eligible items.

### What if a product needs repair?
If a product needs repair within the warranty period, contact the manufacturer's service center. For out-of-warranty repairs, we can help you find authorized service centers.

## Product Recommendations

### How can I find products suitable for my needs?
Use our AI chat assistant! Simply describe what you're looking for, your budget, and preferences. The assistant will recommend the best products for you.

### Can I get personalized product recommendations?
Yes, based on your browsing history, purchase history, and preferences, we provide personalized product recommendations. Log in to see recommendations tailored for you.

### How do I find products within my budget?
Use the price filter on category pages or search results. You can also ask our AI assistant to find products "under [amount]" and it will show relevant options.

### What if I'm looking for a specific brand?
You can filter products by brand using the brand filter on category pages, or search for the brand name directly in the search bar.
